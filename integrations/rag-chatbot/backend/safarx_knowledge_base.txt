# SafarX Knowledge Base

## About SafarX
SafarX is Pakistan's AI-powered peer-to-peer intercity courier delivery platform. We connect senders with verified travelers for safe, fast, and affordable parcel delivery across Pakistan's major cities.

### How SafarX Works
SafarX connects people who need to send packages with travelers who are already making trips between cities. Senders post their parcel delivery requests, and travelers accept bookings for packages they can carry on their existing trips. This creates a win-win situation where senders get affordable and fast delivery, while travelers earn money from their regular journeys.

### Key Benefits
- Affordable: Lower prices than traditional courier services
- Fast: Same-day or next-day delivery options
- Safe: All travelers are KYC-verified with trust scores
- Trackable: Real-time GPS tracking and status updates
- Insured: Escrow payment system protects both parties

## KYC Verification

### What is KYC?
KYC (Know Your Customer) is an identity verification process required for all SafarX users. This ensures the safety and trustworthiness of our platform.

### How to Complete KYC
1. Go to your Profile
2. Tap on "KYC Verification"
3. Upload the following documents:
   - CNIC Front: Clear photo of your national ID card front
   - CNIC Back: Clear photo of your national ID card back
   - Selfie with CNIC: Hold your CNIC next to your face and take a photo
4. Submit the documents
5. Wait for admin approval (usually 24-48 hours)

### KYC Status
- Not Started: You haven't uploaded documents yet
- Pending: Documents are under review by our admin team
- Approved: You're verified and can use all features
- Rejected: Documents were rejected (check rejection reason and resubmit)

### Important Notes
- All users must complete KYC before posting trips or accepting bookings
- Senders can post parcels without KYC approval
- Only KYC-approved travelers can accept booking requests
- Make sure photos are clear and readable
- CNIC must be valid and not expired

## For Senders

### How to Post a Package

1. Go to Sender Dashboard
2. Tap "Post a Package Request" button
3. Fill in package details:
   - Package Title (e.g., "Documents", "Gift Items", "Clothes")
   - Description: What's inside the package
   - Pickup Address: Where the package should be collected from
   - Pickup City: Select from available cities
   - Dropoff Address: Where the package should be delivered
   - Dropoff City: Select destination city
   - Package Weight: Enter weight in kilograms
   - Package Size: Small, Medium, or Large
   - Fragile: Yes/No
   - Value: Approximate value of contents
   - Preferred Date: When you want it delivered
   - Offered Price: How much you're willing to pay
4. Upload package photo (optional but recommended)
5. Review details
6. Submit package request

### Finding Travelers
After posting your package:
1. View "Available Trips" matching your route
2. See traveler profiles with trust scores and reviews
3. Send booking requests to travelers you trust
4. Wait for traveler acceptance

### Booking Process for Senders
1. Post Package Request
2. Browse Available Trips or wait for travelers to find you
3. Send Booking Request to a traveler
4. Traveler accepts or rejects your request
5. If accepted, make payment through the app
6. Payment is held in escrow
7. Traveler picks up your package
8. Track delivery in real-time
9. Confirm delivery once received
10. Payment is released to traveler
11. Leave a review

### Package Pickup
Once a booking is accepted and payment is made:
- Traveler will contact you to arrange pickup time
- Be available at the pickup address
- Hand over the package to the traveler
- Traveler will mark it as "Picked Up" in the app

### Tracking Your Parcel
1. Go to "My Parcels" from Sender Dashboard
2. Tap on your active delivery
3. View delivery timeline showing:
   - Package Posted
   - Booking Accepted
   - Payment Made
   - Picked Up
   - In Transit
   - Delivered
4. See traveler's real-time location (when available)
5. Contact traveler via in-app messaging

### Payment and Pricing
- You set your offered price when posting a package
- Travelers see your offer and can accept it
- Payment is made through Stripe (credit/debit card) or wallet balance
- Payment is held in escrow until delivery is confirmed
- Platform fee: 10% of booking amount
- Refund: If booking is cancelled before pickup, you get full refund

### Delivery Confirmation
When the package arrives:
1. Check the package condition
2. Tap "Confirm Delivery" in the app
3. Payment is released to the traveler
4. Write a review for the traveler

### Cancellation Policy for Senders
- Before Traveler Acceptance: Free cancellation
- After Acceptance, Before Pickup: Cancel with 20% fee
- After Pickup: No cancellation, but you can file a dispute
- Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

## For Travelers

### How to Post a Trip

1. Go to Traveler Dashboard
2. Tap "Post a Trip" button
3. Fill in trip details:
   - Source City: Where you're starting from
   - Destination City: Where you're going to
   - Departure Date: When you're traveling
   - Arrival Date: When you'll reach destination
   - Available Capacity: How much weight you can carry (kg)
   - Vehicle Type: Car, Bus, Train, etc.
   - Trip Description: Any additional information
4. Review and submit

### Finding Package Requests
After posting your trip:
1. Go to "Booking Requests" to see sender requests
2. Browse packages matching your route
3. View package details and offered prices
4. Accept or reject booking requests

### Booking Process for Travelers
1. Post Your Trip
2. Receive booking requests from senders
3. Review package details and sender profile
4. Accept or reject the request
5. Coordinate pickup with sender
6. Pick up the package
7. Mark as "Picked Up" in app
8. Transport package safely
9. Upload delivery proof photo
10. Wait for sender confirmation
11. Receive payment
12. Leave a review for sender

### Package Pickup for Travelers
After accepting a booking:
1. Contact the sender via in-app messaging
2. Arrange a convenient pickup time
3. Go to the pickup address
4. Verify package contents match description
5. Take a photo of the package
6. Mark as "Picked Up" in the app

### During Transit
1. Keep the package safe and secure
2. Do not open or tamper with contents
3. Update delivery status if there are delays
4. Respond to sender messages promptly

### Delivery Process
When you reach the destination:
1. Contact the recipient
2. Arrange delivery time and place
3. Hand over the package
4. Take a delivery proof photo (optional)
5. Tap "Upload Proof" in the app
6. Wait for sender to confirm delivery
7. Once confirmed, your payment is released

### Earnings and Payments
- You receive the full booking amount minus platform fee (10%)
- Payments are held in escrow until delivery is confirmed
- Money is added to your wallet automatically
- You can withdraw wallet balance to your bank account
- Minimum withdrawal: PKR 500
- Withdrawal processing: 1-3 business days

### Trust Score and Reviews
- New travelers start with 5.0 trust score
- Your score updates based on customer reviews
- Higher trust score = more booking requests
- Good reviews help you earn more

## Payments and Wallet

### Payment Methods
SafarX accepts:
- Credit/Debit Cards (via Stripe)
- Wallet Balance

### How Payment Works
1. Sender makes payment after traveler accepts booking
2. Payment is held in escrow (safe holding)
3. Traveler picks up and delivers the package
4. Sender confirms delivery
5. Payment is released to traveler's wallet
6. Traveler can withdraw to bank account

### Wallet
Your SafarX wallet:
- Stores your earnings (for travelers)
- Can be used to pay for deliveries (for senders)
- View balance in "Wallet" section
- Add money to wallet via card payment
- Withdraw to bank account (minimum PKR 500)

### Platform Commission
SafarX charges 10% commission on each completed booking. This fee covers:
- Payment processing
- Platform maintenance
- Customer support
- Insurance and dispute resolution

### Refund Policy
Refunds are issued when:
- Booking is cancelled before pickup (full refund)
- Traveler cancels after acceptance (full refund minus processing fee)
- Package is lost or damaged (case-by-case basis)
- Dispute is resolved in sender's favor
Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to original payment method or wallet.

## Disputes and Support

### When to File a Dispute
File a dispute if:
- Package was not delivered
- Package contents are damaged or missing
- Wrong package was delivered
- Traveler is unresponsive after pickup
- Payment issues

### How to File a Dispute
1. Go to the booking details
2. Tap "Report an Issue" or "File Dispute"
3. Select dispute reason:
   - Not Delivered
   - Damaged Package
   - Missing Items
   - Wrong Package
   - Other
4. Provide details and upload photos if applicable
5. Submit dispute
6. Wait for admin review (24-48 hours)

### Dispute Resolution Process
1. Admin reviews dispute and evidence
2. Both parties may be contacted for information
3. Decision is made based on evidence
4. Refund or compensation issued if applicable

## Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is SafarX available in my city?
A: SafarX currently operates in major Pakistani cities including Karachi, Lahore, Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Faisalabad, Multan, Peshawar, Quetta, Sialkot, and Gujranwala. More cities coming soon!

Q: How long does KYC approval take?
A: KYC approval typically takes 24-48 hours. Make sure to upload clear, readable photos of your CNIC and selfie.

Q: Can I send fragile items?
A: Yes, but mark your package as "Fragile" when posting. Pack items securely and discuss with the traveler before they accept.

Q: What if my package is lost or damaged?
A: File a dispute immediately through the app. Our admin team will review and resolve based on evidence provided.

Q: How do I withdraw money from my wallet?
A: Go to Wallet > Withdraw. Enter amount (minimum PKR 500), provide bank details, and confirm. Money arrives in 1-3 business days.

Q: Can I cancel a booking?
A: Yes, before the traveler picks up the package. Cancellation fees may apply after traveler acceptance. See cancellation policy for details.

Q: Is my payment safe?
A: Yes, all payments are processed through Stripe (international standard) and held in escrow until delivery is confirmed.

Q: Do I need to complete KYC to send packages?
A: Senders can post packages without KYC, but travelers must be KYC-approved to accept bookings.

Q: How is the trust score calculated?
A: Trust score is the average of all ratings you receive from other users. It starts at 5.0 and updates with each new review.

Q: Can I track my package in real-time?
A: Yes, travelers can share their live location, and you can track the delivery status through the app timeline.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?
A: Credit/debit cards via Stripe and wallet balance. We're working on adding more payment options.

Q: What happens if the traveler doesn't deliver my package?
A: File a dispute. If the traveler is found at fault, you'll receive a full refund and the traveler's trust score will be impacted.

## Troubleshooting

### App Issues
Problem: Can't login
Solution: Check your email/username and password. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.

Problem: KYC documents not uploading
Solution: Ensure photos are clear, under 5MB, in JPG/PNG format. Check internet connection.

Problem: Payment failed
Solution: Check card details, ensure sufficient balance. Try a different card or contact your bank.

Problem: Can't see available trips
Solution: Refresh the page. Check if there are active trips matching your route and dates.

Problem: Messages not sending
Solution: Check internet connection. Refresh the app. Contact support if issue persists.

Problem: Wallet withdrawal pending
Solution: Withdrawals take 1-3 business days. If delayed beyond 5 days, contact support.

### Common Solutions
- Restart the app
- Check internet connection
- Update app to latest version
- Clear app cache
- Logout and login again
- Contact support if nothing works
